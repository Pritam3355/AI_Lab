REGULATORY UPDATE
Date: January 2025

Subject: New Margin Rules for Derivatives Trading

1. Initial Margin Increase
Initial margin requirement increased from 10% to 15%.

2. Intraday Leverage Restrictions
Intraday leverage capped at 5x.

3. Reporting Changes
Brokers must report client-level margin data daily.

4. Penalties
Non-compliance may result in fines and suspension.

5. Effective Date
March 1, 2025
