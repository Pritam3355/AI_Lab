Regulatory Obligations Dataset (SEBI / IFSCA / RBI)
================================================================================

Record 1
Regulator: SEBI | Type: Circular
Title: Minimum information for Audit Committee on Related Party Transactions
Obligation: Pre-approval information for RPTs
Summary: Provide detailed information to Audit Committee/shareholders before approving related party transactions, including relationship, value, tenure and justification.
Risk Level: High | Compliance Type: Disclosure & Governance
Frequency/Deadline: Before each RPT approval
Impact if Breached: Potential violation of LODR; enforcement under SEBI Act.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Record 2
Regulator: SEBI | Type: Circular
Title: Cybersecurity and Cyber Resilience for SEBI Regulated Entities
Obligation: Cyber audit and incident reporting
Summary: Implement cyber audit, continuous monitoring, incident reporting and annual Board review for cybersecurity framework.
Risk Level: High | Compliance Type: Operational Risk & Technology
Frequency/Deadline: Annual audit; immediate incident reporting
Impact if Breached: Regulatory action for non-compliance with framework.

Record 3

Title: Measures for risk management in equity derivatives
Obligation: Enhanced margining and monitoring
Summary: Monitor client and member positions, manage concentration, enhance margining and stress testing for index derivatives.
Risk Level: Medium | Compliance Type: Market Risk
Frequency/Deadline: Ongoing daily monitoring
Impact if Breached: Risk of enforcement for inadequate risk controls.

Record 5
Regulator: IFSCA | Type: Regulation
Title: IFSCA (Fund Management) Regulations – FME registration
Obligation: Register FME and maintain eligibility
Summary: Fund Management Entities in IFSC must meet net worth, eligibility, governance and reporting requirements to be registered.
Risk Level: High | Compliance Type: Licensing & Prudential
Frequency/Deadline: Onboarding and ongoing
Impact if Breached: Suspension or cancellation of FME registration.

Obligation: Implement stewardship code
Summary: FMEs must adopt stewardship responsibilities, including voting, engagement and conflict management for investee entities.

Record 8
Regulator: RBI | Type: Master Direction
Title: KYC Master Directions – risk-based KYC updates
Obligation: Risk-based KYC and periodic review
Summary: Banks and NBFCs must categorise customers by risk, perform periodic KYC review and upload standardised KYC data.
Risk Level: High | Compliance Type: AML/CFT & Customer Due Diligence
Frequency/Deadline: High risk: frequent; low risk: longer cycles
Impact if Breached: AML/CFT breaches; monetary penalty, supervisory action.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Record 9
Regulator: RBI | Type: Guideline
Title: Digital lending – explicit consent for data sharing
Obligation: Explicit customer consent for data use
Summary: Digital lenders must obtain explicit customer consent for data sharing with credit bureaus and third parties.
Risk Level: High | Compliance Type: Consumer Protection & Data Privacy
Frequency/Deadline: At onboarding and before each new use
Impact if Breached: License restrictions or cancellation in severe cases.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Record 10
Regulator: RBI | Type: Master Direction
Title: KYC provisions – removal of OTP-based lending cap
Obligation: Align lending policies with new KYC directions
Summary: Institution-specific KYC directions remove prior universal cap on OTP-based e-KYC loans, replacing with risk-based safeguards.
Risk Level: Medium | Compliance Type: Credit Risk & KYC
Frequency/Deadline: Policy update within regulatory timeline
Impact if Breached: Supervisory findings and corrective action.
