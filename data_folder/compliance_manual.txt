COMPLIANCE MANUAL
Capital Markets Intermediary

1. KYC Requirements
All clients must submit valid identity and address proof before onboarding.

2. Anti-Money Laundering (AML)
Transactions above INR 10,00,000 are flagged for review.

3. Trading Compliance
- No insider trading allowed
- Employees must pre-clear trades

4. Risk Management
- Daily margin monitoring
- Exposure limits per client

5. Reporting Obligations
- Monthly reports to regulator
- Suspicious transaction reporting

6. Record Keeping
All trade records must be retained for 7 years.

7. Audit
Internal audit conducted every 6 months.
