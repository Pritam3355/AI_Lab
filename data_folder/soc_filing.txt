SYSTEM AND ORGANIZATION CONTROLS (SOC) REPORT
Capital Markets Brokerage Ltd.

Reporting Period: April 1, 2024 – March 31, 2025

1. Overview
This SOC report outlines internal controls over financial reporting for Capital Markets Brokerage Ltd. The organization provides equity trading, derivatives trading, and portfolio advisory services.

2. Control Environment
The company maintains a strong governance framework led by the Board of Directors and Audit Committee.

3. Risk Assessment
Key risks identified:
- Market volatility risk
- Trade settlement failures
- Cybersecurity breaches

4. Control Activities
- Daily reconciliation of trades
- Automated margin checks
- Segregation of duties between front office and back office

5. Information Systems
Trading systems are monitored 24/7 with redundancy mechanisms.

6. Monitoring Controls
Quarterly internal audits are conducted to ensure compliance.

7. Conclusion
No material weaknesses identified during the reporting period.
